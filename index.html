Where art comes home

Research in Art Ottawa

Do you ever ask yourself what artists can do to resist and change our dysfunctional systems based on profit, exploitation, and competition? The neo-liberal approach to our environment has led to an existential climate crisis that puts life on this planet in critical peril. Climate change calls out for immediate response from all of us according to our talents and capabilities. What can artists do? Ria Links Artists for System Change hopes to bring together critical artists from Ottawa and elsewhere to discuss collective and individual possibilities and generate ideas for action.

Salons in the Cloud

Salons in the cloud are open to anyone, artists and non-artists, interested in, or curious about, art and systems change.

We invite artists from all disciplines, artists’ collectives, and study-groups who share a drive to work for systems change through their art in and around Ottawa, to showcase their projects and discuss their ideas in a Ria Links Salon.

Salons in the Cloud will last as long as we can find presenters.
